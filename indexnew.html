About

I am a second-year master student in Biomedical Engineering in Chalmers. Previously attended MBA in University of Indonesia, and graduated from Bandung Institute Technology in electronics engineering.

I have four year experience in embedded system engineering, electronics, and system design. I also have practical Linux server experience. Using Arduino, Raspberry Pi, and other similar platform, I have built solutions for telemetry, IoT, home automation, and remote sensing.

In my spare time, I pursue my interests in economics and bitcoin to widen my perspective.
